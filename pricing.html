Common Questions

How long does a project take? +

Chatbots and simple automations take 7–10 days. RAG systems take 10–14 days. Full backends take 20–30 days. We'll give you a clear timeline before starting.

Do I get the source code? +

Enterprise clients get full source code. Starter and Growth clients get full access to deploy and use — source code available as an add-on for ₹2,000.

What if I'm not satisfied? +

We do a review call before final delivery. If something isn't right, we fix it. Your satisfaction is the deliverable.

Can I pay in installments? +

Yes — 50% upfront, 50% on delivery. We can discuss other arrangements for larger projects.
